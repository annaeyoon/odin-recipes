Lemon Pepper Shrimp Orzo

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lb shrimp
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1/2 cup chopped shallots
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 1/4 cups orzo
  • 3 cups chicken or vegetable broth
  • 3 cups spinach
  • 1 lemon
  • 2 tbsp parsley

Instructions

  1. Season shrimp with salt and pepper.

    Heat 1 tbsp of butter and olive oil in a pan, cook the shrimp on each side for 3 minutes. Transfer to a plate and keep warm.

  2. Add remaining butter and olive oil to pan. Add the shallots and garlic, cook for 2-3 minutes until aromatic. Add orzo and lightly toast for a couple minutes.

    Add broth, salt and pepper and bring it to a boil. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 10-15 minutes until most of the broth is absorbed.

    During the final few minutes, stir in the spinach. When finished, stir in the lemon juice.

  3. Put the shrimp in the skillet and garnish with parsley.
