Curry Udon

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 3/4 lb sliced pork
  • 1/2 onion
  • 6 cups dashi
  • 4 cubes
  • scallion
  • 2 servings udon
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Make soup base by bringing 6 cups of unsalted dashi to a simmer. Season with 1/2 cup mirin, 1/4 cup shoyu, 1 TB sugar and stir.
  2. Boil udon noodles according to package instructions. Rinse under cold water and drain, then set aside.
  3. In a new pot, saute pork belly until no longer pink. Add sliced onion and saute until it starts to soften.
  4. Pour the dashi broth into the pot to cover the pork and onion. Bring to a boil then reduce heat to a low boil.
  5. Add around 3-4 curry cubes (to taste) and dissolve until soup becomes desired thickness. If too thick, add more water.
  6. Scramble 2 raw eggs in a small separate bowl, then slowly pour into the soup while whisking vigorously with chopsticks.
  7. Pour curry broth over udon noodles, garnish with scallion, and enjoy!
