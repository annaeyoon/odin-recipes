Gochujang Butter Steak

Back to Home

Ingredients

  • 1 NY steak
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 1/4 lemon
  • chives
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 6 shiitake mushrooms, quartered
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/3 cup sake
  • 1 tsp beef bouillon
  • 1 tbsp gochujang

Instructions

  1. Season steak with salt & pepper. Sear in neutral oil in a skillet over high heat until preferred doneness. Set aside to rest.

  2. Using the same pan, set heat to medium and melt butter. Once the butter begins to foam, throw in the garlic.

  3. When garlic is fragrant, deglaze the pan with sake and stir. Let the alcohol cook off for a couple minutes, then add gochujang, beef bouillon, mirin, lemon, and a couple cracks of pepper.

  4. Mix constantly to emulsify the sauce. Loosen with water if necessary.

  5. Reduce heat to low, then toss in your mushrooms. Stir to softly cook the mushrooms and coat with the sauce.

  6. Finally, slice your steak, spoon over the sauce and mushrooms, and garnish with a generous amount of finely chopped chives.
